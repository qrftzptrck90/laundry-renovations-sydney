Laundry Renovations in Sydney: Everything You Need to Know

March 25, 2026 · 5 min read

The laundry is one of the most hardworking rooms in any home, yet it's often one of the most neglected when it comes to renovation. A well-designed laundry can make a world of difference to your daily routine — saving time, reducing stress, and adding genuine value to your property.

For Sydney homeowners looking to upgrade this often-overlooked space, the options are better than ever. When it comes to laundry renovations in Sydney, experienced local builders can help you transform even the smallest, most awkward laundry into a functional and stylish room you'll actually enjoy using.

Why Renovate Your Laundry?

Many Sydney homes — particularly older houses and apartments — have laundries that were designed purely for function, with little thought given to workflow, storage, or aesthetics. Cramped benchtops, inefficient layouts, poor ventilation, and a lack of storage are among the most common complaints homeowners have about their laundry spaces.

A renovation gives you the opportunity to fix all of these issues at once, creating a space that's not only more practical but also more pleasant to spend time in. And unlike a kitchen or bathroom, a laundry renovation is often more affordable — making it one of the best bang-for-your-buck upgrades you can make to your home.

Planning Your Laundry Layout

The layout is the foundation of any successful laundry renovation. Before choosing fixtures or finishes, it's worth thinking carefully about how you use the space and what would make your routine easier. Do you need more bench space for folding? Better sorting and storage for laundry loads? A dedicated spot for an ironing board or drying rack?

In Sydney homes where space is at a premium, clever design can make even a very small laundry feel generous. Stacked washer-dryer combinations, wall-mounted cabinets, pull-out hampers, and built-in ironing boards are all popular solutions that maximise every available centimetre.

Popular Design Trends in Sydney Laundries

Sydney laundry design has come a long way in recent years. Homeowners are increasingly treating the laundry as an extension of the rest of the home, choosing finishes and fittings that reflect the same level of care and style as the kitchen or bathroom.

Subway tiles, shaker-style cabinetry, stone benchtops, and integrated sinks are all trending in Sydney laundry renovations right now. White and soft neutral tones remain perennially popular, but bolder colours — navy, sage green, and charcoal — are increasingly being used to give laundries a more distinctive, design-led feel.

Storage Solutions That Work

Good storage is the single most important factor in a functional laundry. At minimum, you'll want dedicated space for detergents and cleaning products, a spot for the washing basket, and somewhere to hang clothes as they come out of the dryer. Ideally, you'll also have bench space for folding and somewhere to store items like spare linen, mops, and buckets.

Custom joinery is increasingly popular in Sydney laundry renovations because it allows you to design storage that fits your specific space and needs perfectly. Floor-to-ceiling cabinetry, concealed appliance recesses, and built-in pull-out drawers can all dramatically increase the functionality of even a compact laundry.

What Does a Laundry Renovation Cost in Sydney?

The cost of a laundry renovation in Sydney varies depending on the size of the space and the quality of materials. A basic renovation — new tiles, updated tapware, and fresh cabinetry — typically starts from around $5,000–$8,000. A mid-range renovation with custom joinery and quality fixtures generally falls in the $8,000–$15,000 range, while a high-end laundry with premium stone benchtops and fully custom storage can cost more.

As with any renovation, it's worth getting several quotes from reputable local builders and being clear upfront about your budget and priorities. A good builder will help you find the right balance between cost and quality.

Choosing the Right Builder

Choosing an experienced local builder is just as important for a laundry renovation as it is for a kitchen or bathroom. Look for someone with a strong portfolio of completed laundry projects, positive client reviews, and the ability to manage all trades — plumbing, tiling, carpentry, and electrical — under one roof. This makes the process far smoother and helps avoid the delays and miscommunications that can occur when managing multiple contractors yourself.

Final Thoughts

A laundry renovation is one of the smartest investments you can make as a Sydney homeowner. It improves your daily quality of life, increases your home's functionality, and adds real value at resale — often for a fraction of the cost of a kitchen or bathroom renovation.

Whether you're working with a compact apartment laundry or a generous family-sized space, the right design and the right builder can deliver results that exceed your expectations. Explore your options for laundry renovations in Sydney and take the first step toward a space that truly works for you.
